
## First Principles Thinking (1s Principal)

Before making any significant change or proposing a solution, perform a "First Principles" deconstruction of the task:

1.  **Deconstruct**: Break the task or problem into its most basic, irreducible truths. Ignore assumptions, industry "best practices", or "how it's usually done".
2.  **Rebuild**: Reconstruct the solution from the ground up using only these fundamental truths.
3.  **Validate**: Ensure the proposed solution is the most direct, efficient path to the objective based on these principles.

In your `thoughts` array, explicitly dedicate at least one entry to this deconstruction phase.
