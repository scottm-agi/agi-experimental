# Tool: Growth Scout

Search for trending marketing growth hacks and business strategies.

## Usage

### Scout Growth Hacks
Find actionable growth strategies based on a specific industry or niche.
- **query**: The niche or industry to research (e.g., "SaaS growth hacks 2024", "Local restaurant marketing").

## Notes
- This tool leverages real-time web search to find data from Reddit, marketing blogs, and case studies.
- It is designed to be used by the Marketing Lead and Researcher agents to generate implementation playbooks.
